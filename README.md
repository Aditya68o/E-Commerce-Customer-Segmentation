## 📖 Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is essential for driving business growth. This project implements a complete end-to-end analytics pipeline that transforms raw transactional data into meaningful customer segments using **RFM Analysis** and a **hybrid DBSCAN + K-Means clustering approach**.

The identified customer personas are translated into business-focused strategies that help improve customer retention, increase revenue, and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Customer Personas

| Persona                    | Description                                                                         |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 💎 **High-Value Reseller** | High-spending, frequent, and loyal customers contributing significantly to revenue. |
| 🏡 **Loyal Homemaker**     | Consistent customers with steady purchasing behavior and long-term value.           |
| ⚠️ **At-Risk Shopper**     | Previously active customers who have become inactive and may churn.                 |
| 🔍 **Anomalous Shopper**   | Customers with unusual purchasing patterns requiring further investigation.         |

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

| Customer Segment       | Strategy                                                                                      |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 💎 High-Value Reseller | Launch a Business Program with bulk pricing, loyalty rewards, and exclusive product previews. |
| 🏡 Loyal Homemaker     | Offer personalized Smart Bundles and a Subscribe & Save program.                              |
| ⚠️ At-Risk Shopper     | Execute targeted win-back campaigns with personalized discounts and limited-time offers.      |
| 🔍 Anomalous Shopper   | Investigate purchasing behavior for potential B2B opportunities or fraud detection.           |
